About

Hi, I'm Janette Rose.

I am a photographer based in Vancouver, BC. I enjoy capturing the moments in life, whether that's between a wedding, sports event or capturing the world around us. I graduated from the BCIT New Media Design & Web Development Diploma program. I love using my creativity and my design background to capture unique and beautiful images.

Who I am?

I am a freelance photographer. My mission is to capture the wonders of the world and preserve all the important moments in life, celebrating and creating memories of all of our special experiences.

Why choose me?

I go with the flow of people, making sure you are completely comfortable and enjoying the experience. I focus on capturing the action and emotion rather than forcing you into stiff poses. I want to see the real you, so you can look back at the real moments.

What I can do for you?

My services include wedding, sport and nature photography, but I am more than happy to take on group and individual portrait sessions too. All my photos are available for digital and printed package versions. I work together to help out each of my clients to decide the best way to display and protect their precious memories.
